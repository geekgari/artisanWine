About Us

Our wine lounge offers you a tasting bar integrated into separate seating areas for you to enjoy a glass of our wines in a relaxed, warm and friendly environment. Complement your wine with a tasty appetizer. Relax, there’s no rush.

Make a delicious impression with our selection of classy ways to start a meal or a party. Choose from sophisticated, casual and hot appetizers and check out our dips and crowd-pleasing snack mixes.
